How to Play WordFusion

WordFusion is a stylish, engaging game of compound words and natural word pairings.

1

Look at the cards: You are given one word as an anchor (e.g., SWEAT) and a blank series of boxes representing a second, mystery word.

2

Solve the bond: Find the word that forms a compound phrase with the anchor word (e.g., SWEAT + SHOP = Sweatshop).

3

Be careful with clues: If you submit an incorrect guess, or click Reveal Letter, a random hint letter is added to the boxes, but the puzzle's point value decreases by 1!

Scoring Scale

  • 5 pts solved with no hint letters.
  • 4 pts solved with 1 letter revealed.
  • 3 pts solved with 2 letters revealed.
  • 2 pts solved with 3 letters revealed.
  • 1 pt solved with 4 letters revealed.
  • 0 pts skipped or solved by full reveals.
